What is Our Goal?

We want to establish and reinforce a strong test community across digital services.
The purpose of this gathering is to...

  • Network, share and learn together
  • Communicate the desired QA model we have for all projects in Digital Services
  • Equip you with practical ways to implement the model, through crowdsourced tools and practises
  • Give you visibility projects, and the testing happening in them
  • Feedforward in to continual improvement; using the outcomes and observations of gathering to feed into our projects now and in the future
